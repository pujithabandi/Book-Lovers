The Third-Floor Tenant Elena moved into the old apartment building, seeking solitude. The rent was cheap, and the location was convenient. But strange noises echoed from the third floor: - Footsteps at midnight - Whispers in the vents - Doors creaking open Elena dismissed it as the building's age. One evening, she encountered a girl on the third floor: - Pale skin - Sunken eyes - Tattered dress The girl vanished into thin air. Elena researched the building's history: - A family tragedy occurred on the third floor - A young girl died under mysterious circumstances As encounters escalated: - Elena found cryptic messages on her door - The girl's presence grew stronger Desperate for answers, Elena investigated the third floor: - A hidden room revealed the girl's name: Lucy - A series of disturbing Polaroids Elena discovered Lucy's dark past: - Abused and neglected - Died under her father's care Lucy's spirit sought justice. Elena confronted the building's owner, Mr. Jenkins: - He was Lucy's father - Guilt and greed kept him silent As Elena exposed the truth: - Lucy's presence intensified - Jenkins' sanity unraveled In a chilling climax: - Elena and Jenkins faced Lucy's vengeful spirit - Trapped in the hidden room Elena escaped, but Jenkins remained: - Trapped with Lucy's ghost - Forever tortured by his past Elena moved out, but whispers persisted: - The third floor remained haunted - Lucy's presence still lingered
