Love in Full Bloom

Lily, a shy florist, arranged bouquets with love. Finn, a charming customer, ordered flowers weekly for his mother. One day, Finn asked Lily to create a custom bouquet for his mom's birthday. As they worked together, their fingers touched, sparking a connection. Lily blushed. Finn smiled. Their weekly meetings became Lily's highlight. Finn's kindness and humor won her heart. On Valentine's Day, Finn surprised Lily with a bouquet: "You're the flower that makes my life beautiful." Lily's heart bloomed. Finn took Lily's hand, his eyes locked on hers: "I love you, Lily." Lily whispered, "I love you too." As petals fell around them, they shared their first kiss. Epilogue: Lily and Finn's love continued to flourish: They opened a joint flower shop. Their love story inspired sweet gestures. One anniversary, Finn gave Lily a custom bouquet: "Forever yours, my love." Lily smiled, tears shining.
