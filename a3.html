The Bengal Tiger

Raj "The Bengal Tiger" Singh, a former Indian Army sniper, had retired from his life as a hitman for the powerful Mumbai-based crime syndicate, the Khanna Family. But when Raj's younger brother, police officer Rohan Singh, was brutally murdered by a rival gang, the ruthless Kaur Brothers, Raj was pulled back into the underworld. Seeking vengeance, Raj rejoined the Khanna Family, now led by the cunning and beautiful Priya Khanna. Raj's mission: infiltrate the Kaur Brothers' operation and take down their ruthless leader, Vikram Kaur. In the streets of Mumbai, Raj navigated treacherous alliances, corrupt cops, and ancient vendettas. With each enemy he eliminated, Raj drew closer to Vikram. In a thrilling showdown, Raj faced off against Vikram and his deadliest assassin, the notorious "Shadow Dagger." Raj's military training and battle-honed instincts proved superior. Vikram fell, and the Kaur Brothers' empire crumbled. Priya Khanna approached Raj, her eyes locked on his. "Raj, you're the Bengal Tiger reborn. Lead our family's enforcers, and together we'll dominate India's underworld." Raj hesitated, weighing loyalty and morality. "I'll protect our family, but on one condition: we operate with honor." Priya smiled, knowing Raj's code would strengthen their power. The Bengal Tiger's roar echoed through Mumbai's streets once more.
